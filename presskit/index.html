Description

ImaginationOverflow is a team of people that comes together in order to make apps or games. Technically our team consists of two full time developers, but for every project we usually invite other developers, graphical and audio designers to help us make our apps and games. ImaginationOverflow started publishing apps in 2011 and games in 2016.
